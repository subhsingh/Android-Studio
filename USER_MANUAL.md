## üÜò Using Emergency Mode

### When to Use
Use the emergency button when you feel unsafe or threatened:
- Walking alone in an unsafe area
- Being followed
- Feeling threatened or harassed
- Medical emergency
- Accident or injury
- Any situation where you need immediate help

### How to Activate

1. **Hold the SOS button for 2 seconds**
   - The large red button in the center of the home screen
   - You'll feel it vibrate slightly

2. **Confirm Activation**
   - A dialog will appear asking for confirmation
   - Shows what will happen (SMS, call, alarm)
   - Tap **"YES - ACTIVATE"** to proceed
   - Or tap **"Cancel"** if it was accidental

3. **Emergency Mode Activates**
   - Within seconds, the app will:
     1. Get your current GPS location
     2. Send SMS to ALL emergency contacts with:
        - Your custom message
        - Your exact location (Google Maps link)
     3. Call the emergency number (if enabled)
     4. Sound the loud alarm (if enabled)
     5. Vibrate continuously
     6. Show a notification

### What Happens Next

**On Your Device:**
- Emergency call screen appears (if auto-call enabled)
- Alarm plays loudly for 30 seconds
- Phone vibrates in a pattern
- Notification shows "Emergency Mode Active"

**Your Emergency Contacts Receive:**
- SMS message with text like:
  ```
  üö® EMERGENCY! I need help. My current location: 
  https://maps.google.com/?q=28.6139,77.2090
  ```
- They can tap the link to see your exact location on Google Maps
- They can immediately call you back or come to help

### Stopping the Alarm
- The alarm automatically stops after 30 seconds
- Or you can press the volume down button to stop it manually

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: Will the app work without internet?
**A:** Yes! SMS and phone calls work on cellular network. However, the Google Maps link in the SMS requires internet for your contacts to open it.

### Q2: Does the app drain battery?
**A:** No. The app only runs in the background during emergency mode. Normal usage has minimal battery impact.

### Q3: Can I test the emergency feature safely?
**A:** Yes! Follow these steps:
1. Add only your own phone number as a test contact
2. Disable auto-call in settings
3. Turn off alarm to avoid loud sounds
4. Activate emergency and check if you receive the SMS
5. Remember to restore actual contacts after testing!

### Q4: What if I accidentally activate emergency?
**A:** When you hold the button, a confirmation dialog appears. Just tap "Cancel". If you did activate it, immediately call your contacts to let them know it was accidental.

### Q5: Can I use this app in other countries?
**A:** Yes! Just change the emergency number in settings to match the local emergency number:
- India: 112
- USA: 911
- UK: 999
- Europe (most): 112

### Q6: How accurate is the location?
**A:** Very accurate (within 5-50 meters) if GPS is enabled. Make sure location services are turned on and you're in an open area for best accuracy.

### Q7: Will emergency contacts get multiple messages?
**A:** Each contact gets one SMS per emergency activation. The app doesn't spam.

### Q8: Can I customize the alarm sound?
**A:** Currently, the app uses the system default alarm. Custom alarm sounds may be added in future versions.

### Q9: What if my phone is locked?
**A:** You need to unlock your phone first to access the app and activate emergency mode. Consider using quick launch methods (see Tips & Tricks).

### Q10: Is this app free?
**A:** Yes! Suraksha Kavach is completely free with no hidden charges.
